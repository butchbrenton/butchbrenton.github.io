Reviewing the data it is not clear if there is a relationship between opioid overdoses and heroin/fentanyl overdoses. #read in data
df_pres = pd.read_csv ('presidents.csv') 

#create dictionary of counts
num = df_pres['State']
num = dict(num.value_counts())
#set x and y data
numx = num.keys()
numy = num.values()
#plot bar chart data to make histogram
fig, ax = plt.subplots()
ax.bar(numx,numy)
fig.set_size_inches(7,5)
fig.suptitle('Histogram of US Presidents birth State')
ax.xaxis.set_label_text('Birth State')
plt.xticks(rotation=270)
ax.yaxis.set_label_text('Number of Presidents')
plt.show()

A Histogram was chosen to determine in which states the greatest number of US presidents were born. A histogram is the best way to display frequency of occurence.

Question:

  1. Which states are associated with the greatest number of United States presidents in terms of the presidents birthplace?

The US State with the greatest number of presidents born there is Virginia with eight, followed closely by Ohio where seven presidents were born. Only four states (Virginia, Ohio, New York, and Massachusetts) had more than two presidents born there.

df_sales = pd.read_csv ('TOTALNSA.csv')

fig, ax = plt.subplots()
# plot line graph
ax.plot(df_sales['DATE'], df_sales['TOTALNSA'])
fig.suptitle('United States Vehicle Sales per Month')
ax.xaxis.set_label_text('Date')
ax.yaxis.set_label_text('Number of Vehicles Sold')

fig.set_size_inches(15,5)
plt.show()

A Line graph was selected to show the number of vehicles sold each month over time. A line graph is particularly useful when looking at time series date because trends over time can be quickly seen.

Question:

  1. How have vehicle sales in the United States varied over time?

The US vehicle sales since 1976 has been cyclic over time, staying within a consistent range. There are periods of increasing sales are followed by periods of decreasing sales that follow the macroeconomic conditions but the overall trend has been relatively flat. Along with the macro trends there is also an annual cycle of sales which remains fairly consistent year to year.
